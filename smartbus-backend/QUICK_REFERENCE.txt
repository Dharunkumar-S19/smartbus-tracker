📍 WHAT IT DOES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  Search buses using ANY 2 stops on the route, not just start/end points
  
  Examples:
    • Kinathukadavu → Ukkadam          ✅ Works (10 buses)
    • Kondampatti → Eachanari          ✅ Works (10 buses)
    • Malumichampatti → Town Hall      ✅ Works (10 buses)
    • Sri Eshwar College → Rathinam    ✅ Works (10 buses)
